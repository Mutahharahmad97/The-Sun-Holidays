Privacy Policy

The Sun Holidays

Our privacy policy has been drafted in compliance with the UK Data Protection Act 2018. The Sun Holidays (“we” “us” “our”) care about your privacy and have sworn to protect your data by not sharing, disclosing or selling it. This privacy policy explains how we collect your personal information and what we do with your personal information. You are enjoined to take some minutes to carefully read this privacy policy to have a better understanding of how we process your data. 

Data Controller

The Data controller in respect of your data is:

United Kingdom

Updates

Our Privacy policy is not cast in stone and it is for this reason that we may in our exclusive discretion to periodically update our privacy policy. We may also update our privacy policy to comply with the provision of data protection laws where necessary. When we update our privacy policy according to any of the two instances, we shall inform you of the updates by publishing the latest privacy policy on our website.

Information we collect from you

We collect the following information from you because you provide it to us: 

Email

You provide us with your email address when you subscribe to our newsletter to get information on the latest holiday deals. You also provide us with your email address when you get in touch with us to ask us questions about our services. 

Other information we technically collect

Access and usage to certain features of our site allow us to routinely collect certain information such as your geographical location, time zone, connection speed, ISP, domain name, and usage type information using a proprietary IP address, mobile device GPS, log data, unique identifiers and device information. If you do not wish us to collect this information, you should deactivate or otherwise control the use of location service through the settings feature of your device. However, if you deactivate the location service certain features of our website might not function properly.

Web Beacon

We use web beacons to monitor your activities on our site for purposes of web analytics. A web beacon is a transparent graphic image usually 1pixel x 1 pixel in size which is placed on our website to monitor your activities when you visit our site or send us an email through the “Get in touch” button on our website. We often use web beacons and cookies together.

You also have rights:

You also have rights emanating from the personal information you provided us with and the information we technically collected from you. Your data protection rights are:

1. The right to ask for your data. 
2. The right to rectify your data and request the rectification to be effected. 
If we are unable to rectify your data, we shall inform you and proffer explanations on our inability to rectify your data.  
3. The right to request that your data be wiped-out. 
4. The right to data portability.
5. The right to request a restriction on the processing of your data.

You can ask us to stop using your data for direct promotional emails by unsubscribing to our newsletters. We will stop immediately when we receive your request. All requests in respect of your rights shall be processed within one month except where your request is complex in which case we shall communicate our estimated period of response within one month. All requests in respect of your rights should be sent to the email address provided in the Data Controller section. 

Links to other websites

This site contains links to other third party websites where users can book flights, hotels and tours as well as purchase flight tickets. Our newsletters also contain third party links which lead users to websites where users can book and pay for flights, hotels and tours.  When you click on those links you will effectively leave our website to the third-party website. While the Sun Holidays recommends and suggests holiday destinations, top city break destinations and culture destinations to visit and super holiday deals inclusive of accommodation, it is not affiliated to those third-party sites that offer the ticket, tours and hotels or accommodations for you. Accordingly, the Sun Holidays cannot be held liable for any personal data breach that occurs when you visit the site. Kindly read the privacy policy of those sites to understand the type of information they collect and how they process and keep your information secure. 

Legal Basis for Processing Your Information 

The legal basis for processing your personal information are:
a) You consented to us using your information in a certain way. 
b) The use is in accordance with a legal duty, or
c) The use is obligatory to shield your pertinent interests or interests of others; or
d) We have a valid interest in using your information–by way of example to provide an enhanced user experience. 

How we use your information

We use your information in the following ways:
• To provide our services of travel destination recommendations to you;
• For marketing and advertising purposes in order to develop and send you promotional materials;
• To timely respond to your queries through our customer care services. 

Sharing your information

We have a “no sell” “no lease” policy on your data. We also do not release your data to the public. However, there are certain instances where we transfer or share your personal information:
• We divulge and sometimes transfer your personal information to third parties who are affiliated with us or our service providers who provide email services to us.
• For marketing purposes- With third parties for joint marketing purposes.
• In response to legal proceedings or regulatory authorities – to comply with the law, a judicial proceeding, subpoena, court order, or other legal processes.

Retention

We retain your data for as long as it is needed to provide our travel destination recommendation services to you and in compliance with applicable laws. Where we no longer provide our services to you, your data shall be retained for such a period as required by law and afterwards, it shall be mechanically erased. 

Data Safety

We use advanced technology to keep your data safe and we shall continually make tremendous efforts to secure your data, however, the internet is not immune to a security breach. Consequently, if there is a security breach to your data, we shall:
• Take steps to investigate it;
• Report to appropriate authorities and;
• Notify you if your data was breached. 

Cross-border transfers of information

We do not transfer your information to a country outside the European Union (EU). 

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not for children so we do not collect children’s personal information. In the unlikely event that we collected a child’s personal information, we shall delete it as soon as we are aware of such data. Kindly contact us, if you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with his/her data.
